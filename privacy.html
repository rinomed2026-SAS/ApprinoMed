Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how RINOMED 2026 ("we", "our", or "the Event") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application (the "App") for the International Congress of Rhinology and Otolaryngology 2026.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you register for the App, we collect:

  • Name: Used for personalization and certificate generation
  • Email address: Used for authentication and communication
  • Password: Securely encrypted and stored for account access
  • Role/Category: Attendee, Speaker, Staff, or Admin designation

1.2 Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about how you use the App:

  • Session favorites and personalized agenda selections
  • Questions submitted during Q&A sessions
  • Interaction with sponsors and exhibitors
  • App navigation patterns and feature usage
  • Device information (type, operating system, app version)

1.3 Information We DO NOT Collect

The App does NOT request or collect:

  • ❌ Camera or photo access
  • ❌ Location or GPS data
  • ❌ Microphone or audio recordings
  • ❌ Contact list or address book
  • ❌ Files or documents from your device
  • ❌ Biometric data (fingerprint, face ID)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

2.1 Core Functionality

  • Authenticate your identity and provide secure access to the App
  • Display personalized content, including your favorites and custom agenda
  • Enable you to ask questions during sessions
  • Generate attendance certificates with your name and participation details

2.2 Event Management

  • Track session attendance and engagement for CME/CEU credit reporting
  • Facilitate communication between speakers and attendees
  • Provide event organizers with anonymized analytics and insights

2.3 Improvement & Support

  • Improve App performance and fix technical issues
  • Respond to your support requests and inquiries
  • Send important event updates and notifications

3. Information Sharing & Disclosure

3.1 We DO Share With:

  • Event Organizers: Your name, email, role, and attendance data for event management
  • Speakers: Your questions (with your name) submitted during Q&A sessions
  • Sponsors: If you explicitly interact with a sponsor (e.g., request information), we may share your contact details
  • Certificate Authorities: Your name and attendance data for verification purposes

3.2 We DO NOT Share With:

  • ❌ Third-party advertisers or marketing companies
  • ❌ Data brokers or aggregators
  • ❌ Social media platforms (unless you explicitly choose to share content)
  • ❌ Any party for purposes unrelated to the Event

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government regulation, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of the Event, its attendees, or others.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between the App and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Password Protection: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with salt
  • Token-Based Authentication: JWT tokens with short expiration times and secure refresh rotation
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored in protected databases with restricted access
  • Regular Security Audits: We perform periodic security reviews and updates

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained for the duration of the Event (April 17-18, 2026) plus 60 days for certificate access
  • Session Favorites: Deleted 30 days after the Event concludes
  • Q&A Questions: Retained for 90 days for archival and speaker follow-up purposes
  • Certificates: Available for download for 1 year after the Event

You may request account deletion at any time by contacting us at privacy@rinomed2026.com.

6. Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

6.1 Access & Portability

Request a copy of your personal data in a machine-readable format.

6.2 Correction

Update or correct inaccurate information through the App's profile settings or by contacting us.

6.3 Deletion

Request deletion of your account and associated data (subject to legal retention requirements).

6.4 Objection

Object to processing of your data for specific purposes (e.g., marketing communications).

6.5 Withdrawal of Consent

Withdraw consent for data processing at any time (may limit App functionality).

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for medical professionals and adults attending the Event. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 18 years of age. If we discover we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, we will delete it promptly.

8. International Data Transfers

The Event is hosted in Colombia, and your data may be processed and stored on servers located in Colombia or other countries. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries that may have different data protection laws than your country of residence.

9. Third-Party Services

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services (e.g., hotel booking sites, maps). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. Please review their privacy policies before providing them with your information.

10. Push Notifications

With your permission, we may send push notifications for:

  • Session reminders for your favorited agenda items
  • Important event announcements
  • Q&A responses from speakers

You can disable push notifications in your device settings at any time.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the new policy in the App with an updated "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an in-app notification or email
  • Requiring re-acceptance for material changes

Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), our legal bases for processing personal data are:

  • Contractual Necessity: Processing is necessary to provide the App services
  • Legitimate Interests: Improving the App and Event experience
  • Consent: For optional features like push notifications
  • Legal Obligations: Compliance with applicable laws

13. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the "sale" of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights
